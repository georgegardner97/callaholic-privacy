Support — Callaholic

For help with the app or to report a problem.

Callaholic is a small iPhone app that suggests three fellows to call each day and lets you log the outcome of each call. The most reliable way to get help is to email george.gardner480@gmail.com. Replies usually come within a few days.

How to use Callaholic

Tagging fellows

From the Fellows tab, tap + to add a contact from your iPhone Contacts as a fellow. Callaholic also offers to scan your contacts for an indicator marker — by default "AA" anywhere in the name, plus the first-name-and-last-initial anonymity convention — so you can tag everyone at once.

Daily picks

The Today screen shows three fellows to call. One Newcomer (under 90 days sober by default), one Old Timer (fifteen years or more by default), and one in between. Tap Call to dial. Tap Log to record the outcome — Completed, Voicemail, or No answer.

Streak

Make a call on any given day and the streak counter ticks up. Miss a day and it resets. The streak lives on your device.

Daily reminder

Settings → Daily reminder. Off by default. The reminder fires locally on your phone — no servers involved.

Frequently asked

The scan isn't finding anyone

Check Settings → Indicators. By default Callaholic looks for "AA" in a contact's name, but you can add any marker you use — a meeting name, an emoji, your own shorthand. The scan also recognises the first-name-and-last-initial anonymity convention (e.g. "John B" or "Sarah K"), which you can toggle off in Settings.

I gave Limited Access by mistake — can I change it?

Yes. iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Contacts → Callaholic. From there you can switch between Full Access and Limited Access, or share specific contacts.

How do I remove a fellow?

Open the Fellows tab, tap the fellow, scroll to the bottom of the detail screen, and tap "Remove fellow tag". This removes the tag in Callaholic — your iPhone Contacts are untouched.

Can I sync between iPhone and iPad?

Not yet. A future version may offer optional iCloud sync — private CloudKit, opt-in, off by default. For now, everything lives on the one device you installed the app on.

The app crashed / something looks broken

Email george.gardner480@gmail.com with a short description of what you were doing when it happened. If you can include the iPhone model and iOS version, that helps a lot. Screenshots welcome.

Privacy

Callaholic stores everything on your device. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no advertising. Full details in the privacy policy.
